The order of 1886 Characters

 

    <

 

Characters: Sir Galahad: “An expert tracker and hunter whose instincts and skills, developed over the years fighting Half-Breeds, are legendary among members of The Order. Galahad is world-weary with a calm, cool, professional demeanor.” (playstation)

Sir Perceval/ Sebastien Malory- is a mentor and long-time friend to Galahad, and one of the few Knights who can match Galahad’s strength and ability. One of the eldest knights in the Order, he commands everyone’s respect and is ready to work outside the bounds of the law when a situation calls for it. (playstation)

Lady Igraine/ Isabeau D’Argyll: Under the tutelage of Galahad, Lady Igraine became the youngest member to ever be inducted into The Order. Now, as one of their most skilled and headstrong Knights, both she and Galahad share a mutual respect for each other. (playstation)

Story
FOUNDED IN 2035, THE ATLAS CORPORATION HAS EXPERIENCED A METEORIC RISE TO POWER UNDER THE CONTROL OF ITS CEO, JONATHAN IRONS. 

ORIGINALLY A MERCENARY-FOR-HIRE COMPANY, THINGS CHANGED DRAMATICALLY WHEN IRONS ACQUIRED THE LARGEST BIOENGINEERING R&D FIRM IN THE WORLD AND SPURRED THE DEVELOPMENT OF ADVANCED SOLDIER EXOSKELETON TECHNOLOGY.  ATLAS DEPLOYS A PROPRIETARY COMBINATION OF STATE OF THE ART TRAINING AND ADVANCED MILITARY TECHNOLOGY, AND MAINTAINS THE LARGEST STANDING ARMY IN THE WORLD.

WITH UNMATCHED RESOURCES AND NO CITIZENS TO FEAR, ATLAS CHANGED THE FACE OF WAR AND THE DEFINITION OF POWER, BECOMING A LETHAL AND EFFECTIVE ASSET TO ANY COUNTRY OR CAUSE THAT COULD AFFORD THEM.

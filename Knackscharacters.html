 "Knack is a character made up of relics.  You play as Knack    through out the game.  There are different versions of him you  play as throughout the game such as the Abominable Knack  (Knack made out of ice).  You can even collect different  gems/relics so when you replay the game, you can play as a  different Knack.  Think along the lines of a character skin.

 

Not much is known about Knack other than he is made out of  relics that are found throughout the world the game takes place  in.  One day the Professor finds ruins with relics of blue, red, and  gold color with a golden orb right in the center of the room.  When  the Professor moves towards the orb, the orb floats towards him.  Years go by and the Professor works to find out how to make  the relics and the orb's energy to make a weapon.  That weapon is Knack." (Playstation.com)


 

 

Also known as the "Doctor"-Also known as the Doctor.  Simply put, he is the creator of Knack.  He was the one who found the golden orb and harnessed its energy as well as the relics to make Knack.
